What is Fixed Teeth in a Day?

Fixed teeth in a day, also known as immediate load implants, is a procedure where dental implants and a temporary prosthetic are placed in a single visit.

This approach, often called All-on-4 or All-on-6, allows patients to leave the clinic with functional teeth the same day. It is typically used for patients with significant tooth loss, providing an alternative to dentures by anchoring a full arch of teeth to 4-6 implants.

Applications

All-on-4 / All-on-6

Full arch restoration using 4-6 strategically placed implants to support a complete set of fixed teeth. Ideal for patients with significant tooth loss.

Missing Anterior Teeth

This approach can also be used for missing front teeth where the implant is placed and a fixed temporary tooth is placed in the same appointment.

Denture Alternative

An excellent alternative to traditional removable dentures, providing permanent fixed teeth that don't slip or require daily removal.

Immediate Results

Walk out with functional, beautiful teeth in just one visit. No more waiting months for your new smile.

Important Consideration

In few cases where the bone is less in quantity or quality, fixed teeth can't be given in a day and removable teeth are given in the next few days. This ensures proper healing and optimal results for your implants.

The Procedure

Consultation & Planning

Comprehensive examination, 3D imaging, and detailed treatment planning to determine the best approach for your needs.

Implant Placement

Under local or general anesthesia, 4-6 implants are strategically placed in the jawbone.

Temporary Teeth Attached

A temporary fixed prosthetic is attached to the implants on the same day, allowing you to leave with functional teeth.

Final Restoration

After 2-4 months of healing, the final permanent zirconia or porcelain restoration is placed.
