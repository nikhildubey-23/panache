Precision Restorative Solutions

Advanced crown and bridge work for perfect function and aesthetics

Fixed Partial Dentures (FPD) and dental bridges are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. These custom prosthetic devices are designed to restore your smile's function and appearance, allowing you to eat, speak, and smile with confidence.

Our advanced techniques and high-quality materials ensure optimal fit and durability. Whether you need to replace a single tooth or multiple teeth, our precision-crafted solutions provide the foundation for a healthy, functional smile.

Types of Restorations

Porcelain Crowns

All-ceramic crowns offering superior aesthetics and durability for front teeth.

Zirconia Crowns

Ultra-strong zirconium crowns perfect for posterior teeth and patients who grind their teeth.

Emax Crowns

Lithium disilicate glass-ceramic crowns with exceptional translucency and aesthetics.

Porcelain Fused to Metal

Traditional porcelain fused to metal crowns combining beauty with strength.

Gold Crowns

Timeless, durable option for back teeth and long-lasting results.

Composite Crowns

Cost-effective solution with good aesthetics for front teeth.

Bridge Options

Traditional Bridges

Conventional bridges using adjacent teeth as support, replacing 1-3 missing teeth.

Cantilever Bridges

Modern bridges with precision attachments for secure fit without damaging healthy teeth.

Maryland Bridges

Durable resin-retained bridges offering excellent aesthetics at lower cost than porcelain.

Implant-Supported Bridges

Bridges anchored by dental implants for maximum stability and bone preservation.

When is Crown & Bridge Treatment Needed?

Missing Teeth

Gaps in your smile from missing teeth can affect appearance and function.

Severely Damaged Teeth

Extensive decay or broken teeth beyond repairable with fillings.

Failed Large Fillings

When existing fillings have cracked, fallen out, or developed recurrent decay.

Worn Teeth

Age-related wear causing enamel erosion and appearance changes.

Bite Problems

Misaligned teeth causing uneven wear and jaw joint issues.

The Crown & Bridge Process

Comprehensive Consultation

Detailed examination and discussion of your goals, medical history, and aesthetic preferences.

Tooth Preparation

Gentle reshaping of adjacent teeth to receive crowns or bridges.

Impressions & Scanning

Digital or traditional impressions for precise crown and bridge fabrication.

Temporary FPD

Provisional restoration while permanent restorations are being created.

Final Placement

Precise cementation and adjustments for perfect fit and bite.

Final Check-up

Follow-up appointments to ensure long-term success.
