The Digital Smile Design Process

Facial Analysis

Comprehensive facial assessment with digital photography and measurements to analyze your unique features and create aesthetic baseline.

Dental Evaluation

Thorough examination of your teeth, gums, and bite to understand dental health and treatment possibilities.

3D Digital Scanning

Advanced digital imaging captures precise tooth measurements and creates accurate 3D models of your current dentition.

Smile Design Creation

Custom design of your new smile using specialized software, considering facial harmony, tooth proportions, and your preferences.

Digital Preview

See your new smile from all angles with 3D simulations and mockups before any treatment begins.

Treatment Planning

Final treatment plan developed based on digital design, ensuring predictable outcomes for veneers, crowns, or implants.
